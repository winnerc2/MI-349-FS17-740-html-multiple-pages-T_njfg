Xcom 2

Xcom 2 is a turn based strategy game in which you get to command a rebel group rebelling against the alien leadership which took over Earth following the events of Xcom 1. At it's center is a system centered around gaming an RNG roll. The majority of attacks in this game have a percent chance to miss. Many things come into play to determine the odds of succeeding. The fun and challenge to this game is minimizing the chances of harm coming to your troops in every possible outcome possible. Gamble in the wrong way and a solider can die in one enemy turn easily, and when that happens they are gone forever. Even wounds will put your soldier out of commission for a period in which a rookie will have to try to pull their weight. Which thanks to the level / perk system is a huge liability. Not to mention the fact that the rookies ineptness could very well get another one of your soliders hurt themselves. Not for the faint of heart, but a very rewarding and replayable strategy to be sure.

8 / 10
